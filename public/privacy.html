Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DentalCloud ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our dental-clinic management application on the web and desktop. By using the Service you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — name, email, clinic name, and login credentials you provide when registering.
  • Clinic & clinical data — the records you choose to enter, such as patients, dental charts, treatments, appointments, payments, photos, and lab orders.
  • Payment information — when you subscribe, payment is handled by our authorized payment provider. We do not store full card numbers; we receive only confirmation and limited billing details needed to activate your plan.
  • Usage & technical data — basic technical information needed to operate the Service securely (for example, session and error information).

2. How we use information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To process subscriptions and activate your plan.
  • To provide support and respond to your requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use patient records for advertising.

3. Patient data & your responsibility

You (the clinic or practitioner) are the controller of any patient or clinical data you enter. We process and store this data on your behalf so you can manage your practice. You are responsible for obtaining any consent required by law and for using patient data lawfully and ethically.

4. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored securely with reputable cloud infrastructure providers and is encrypted in transit. Access is restricted to your account. Some data may also be cached locally on your device to let the app work smoothly.

5. Service providers

We rely on trusted third parties to operate the Service, including cloud hosting and database providers and our payment provider. These providers process data only as needed to deliver their services and under their own security and privacy obligations.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information and recommend you keep your password confidential.

7. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us, subject to any legal retention requirements.

8. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and you may export your clinic data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

9. Children

The Service is intended for dental professionals and clinics, not for use by children. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children as end users.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email support@dentalcloud.app.
